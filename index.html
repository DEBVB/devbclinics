Your Coach
Built to Coach.
Here to Develop.
Grant Shieh
Head Coach · DE Boys Volleyball · Royal Legends Club

Grant played three years as a varsity libero before continuing as a libero with Temple's club volleyball program. Time spent owning the back row — reading attacks, directing coverage, competing every rep — is the foundation everything he coaches comes from.

He started coaching at Council Rock North in 2021, working with both the boys and girls programs through 2023. He joined Downingtown East as girls assistant coach in 2023, then took over as head coach of the DE Boys program in 2024. He currently coaches with Royal Legends club as well.

Whether you're training for indoor 6s or developing your sand game, every session is structured, purposeful, and built around where you actually are — not a one-size-fits-all template.
